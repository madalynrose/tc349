Rabbits

I have two rabbits, and caring for them has been an incredibly fun adventure for me. They are much more complex characters than most people expect, and are relatively low maintenance. I love Toby and Bella dearly and will continue to learn more about them in the coming years.

Cooperative Living

This is my third year living in the Michigan State University Student Housing Coops. I think this is the perfect living situation for most people and recommend it to anyone who doesn't want to live in the dorms but still wants a full college experience.
